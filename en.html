Favran Logistics, founded in 2021, is one of the fastest growing logistics companies in Central Asia. We provide third-party logistics (3PL) services to businesses that want to grow or gain a competitive edge in the marketplace, increasing their profitability through the flexibility and efficiency of supply chains without geographical restrictions. WHAT IS 3PL?

3PL is a service that allows you to outsource operational logistics from warehousing to delivery, which ultimately allows you to focus on other parts of your business. Most of the world's fastest growing brands choose 3PL, fully integrated end-to-end supply chain solutions that contribute to long-term business growth.

ADVANTAGES OF WORKING WITH FAVRAN LOGISTICS

Reduced prices

3PLs have more leverage over freight companies than individual shippers. Working on behalf of multiple clients, 3PLs can negotiate prices based on volume and frequency of orders.

Customer Service Quality

We will prepare your goods for transportation in accordance with all international requirements. We will check the integrity of the package, the availability of all necessary accompanying documents. We will quickly and inexpensively deliver it to its destination.

Entering new markets

With Favran logistics, you do not need to invest in your own storage facilities, transport, or hire a whole staff of qualified personnel.

Legal support

Favran logistics will take care of documentation, customs, duties and other issues that arise at the borders, as well as during transportation. Your deliveries will be made with the utmost care. In addition, you save time on the development of complex rules related to different countries.
